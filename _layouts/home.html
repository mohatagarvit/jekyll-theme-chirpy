I am currently working as a Data Scientist at SAP Labs India, Bengaluru. I completed my undergraduate (B.Tech) from Department of Electrical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology (B.H.U) Varanasi. My B. Tech. project was based on Applciations of Probabilistic Graphical Models under the guidance of Prof. K.V. Srinivas and Prof. Sandip Ghosh. I also worked in LISA Lab with Prof. Kunal Narayan Chaudhury on Computer Vision. For more details, please refer to my CV.

My primary research interests are in Machine Learning, Optimization and Operations Research. Currently, I am excited about the potential of probabilistic and graphical methods in Neural Networks such as Graph Neural Networks, Message Passing Neural Networks, Belief Propagation Neural Networks. I am particularly interested in their applications in decision-making and medical diagnosis.

Using the principles from Cal Newton's Deep Work as my guide, I intend to approach my graduate studies with the same fervor and commitment as he did. I like to use my learnings from Charles Duhigg's The Power of Habit to observe habits and routines in nature and organizations and try to develop ones that best suit me.
